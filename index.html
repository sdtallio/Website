Mink

Real mink lashes come from the Mink tail, giving you a light, fluffy, soft natural look. More bold and slightly glossier.

x

Faux Mink

Man-made poly-fibres, designed to imitate real mink fur. They are very fine, soft, and silky, just like real fur. However, they have been imporved, in that if you wet them, they won't lose their curl.

x

Silk

Mid-weight, finer and more flexible then the synthetic type. Silk lashes have that rich black colour therefor best used for special occations. Silk are known to be the least comfortable type of lash extension.

x

Synthetic

Made from a polished, acrylic material and are the firmest/sturdiest of all. Most suitable for those with healthy, dense lashes of their own. Because they are heavier in weight they are not recommended for long-term use.
